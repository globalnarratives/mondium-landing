Let's talk.

Cypher is in closed development. If the problem we're solving is one you recognize, we want to hear from you.

We are looking for the right conversations, not the most conversations.

Mondium is not currently seeking broad public engagement. What we are interested in is contact from people who work directly with political event data — and who have felt, firsthand, the limitations of the tools currently available.

That includes analysts, researchers, risk professionals, and technologists. It also includes potential partners and institutional collaborators who see the infrastructure case for what CIE makes possible.

Political risk & intelligence Analysts working with event data at volume who have built workarounds for the absence of a structured encoding layer.
Defense & government Institutional users of structured political information with requirements around auditability, source credibility, and systematic comparability.
Quantitative & financial Teams that treat political events as inputs to models and have absorbed the normalization burden that unstructured news imposes.
Academic & research Researchers working on political event datasets, conflict databases, or cross-jurisdictional comparative analysis.
